SmartMoney Phase 2 Fix Deployment:

1. Unzip this package into your repo root.
2. Make sure you keep the structure:
   - index.html
   - transactions.html
   - netlify/functions/get_balances.js
   - netlify/functions/get_transactions.js
3. Commit and push to GitHub.
4. Netlify will auto-deploy.
5. Ensure your environment variables are set in Netlify:
   PLAID_CLIENT_ID
   PLAID_SECRET
   PLAID_ENV
   PLAID_ACCESS_TOKEN
